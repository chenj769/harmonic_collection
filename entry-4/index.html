Post-production
Layout レイアウト The layout is the first step to creating Genga 原画 or Key Animation. An animation layout contains the rough animation which is drawn in accordance to the E-konte. Information about camerawork or instructions to compositors are commonly featured on the Layout. The layout also contains a timesheet タイムシート that the animator will fill out. Timesheets account for every frame, camerawork directions and sound. After the layout is submitted, a series of checks and corrections is done by either:
  1. Episode Director 演出, Enshutsu
  2. Director 監督, Kantoku
  3. Sakuga Director 作画監督, sakuga kantoku
  4. Chief Sakuga Director 総作画監督, Sou Sakuga Kantoku
    5. The Chief Sakuga Director is also usually the Character designer and has the final say.
GENGA 原画 Genga is the Key Animation, where it consists of Layout and Tiedown. The animation work is created in Layout and the corrections are applied and tied-down drawings are made.
